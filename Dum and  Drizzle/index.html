Prawns Biryani

Prawn biryani is a flavorful dish made with prawns and basmati rice, layered and cooked with aromatic spices. It can be prepared using the dum technique, which involves a heavy-bottomed vessel with a lid to seal in the flavors. The prawns are typically marinated with spices and caramelized onions, adding a lovely flavor to the dish. Saffron milk is often added for extra color and aroma. Prawn biryani is a popular variation of the traditional biryani, enjoyed on holidays or weekends with family and friends.
